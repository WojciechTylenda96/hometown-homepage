Visit Olsztyn

Enjoy interesting attractions and beautiful views in the capital of Warmia and Mazury.

Top three activities to do at Olsztyn

Olsztyn Planetarium

Built in tribute to the great astronomer - Nicolaus Copernicus - the Planetarium and Observatory in Olsztyn is a great astronomy lesson for young and old tourists. The starry sky, astronomical phenomena, historical instruments and many other engaging attractions will keep anyone interested in the fascinating world of astronomy entertained for many hours. Be sure to take your toddler here - let him discover his passion for learning!

Lakes near Olsztyn

If you are going to the heart of Warmia and Mazury, your holiday plan must include lakes! Olsztyn is surrounded by them on all sides - within its borders there are as many as 16 natural water reservoirs. Lake Ukiel in the north-western part of the city, Lake Redykajny in the north and Lake Długie in the west offer not only beautiful views, but also numerous attractions, such as sailing, diving, kayaking and wakeboarding.

Forest Arboretum

The Forest Arboretum of Warmia and Mazury in Kudypy, located near Olsztyn, is the only botanical garden in the entire voivodeship. Created in a unique place, after a hurricane that hit the area in 1983, today it attracts hundreds of specimens of native and collector's plants. Although it looks most impressive in spring, the Forest Arboretum in Kudypy can be visited at any time of the year.
